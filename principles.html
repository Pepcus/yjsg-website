Our Principles

We are a group of enthusiastic, motivated Jains who believe in Jainism and its principle. We also believe that Jainism is the way to attain ultimate bliss and take off all the worldly pain. By the right belief, knowledge and conduct one can make himself happy. To achieve this, people must have the right knowledge to apply in their life.

  • We want to spread Jainism's key concepts to all Jains. 
  • Our mission is to present Jainism in easy, fun, easily accessible form.
  • We want to utilize Technology at its fullest level in imparting religious education
  • We do not have any monetary motives to run this mission. The only mission is “People Welfare”
  • Create future leaders who possess strong ethical & moral standards.
