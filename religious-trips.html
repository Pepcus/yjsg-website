RELIGIOUS TRIPS

For utilizing the holidays time in fruitful manner, YJSG organizes yatra to different ancient & magnificient "Jain Tirths". The day starts with Poojan, Abhishek, Swadhyay followed by Jain Games & Bhaktis. People of all age enjoy and learn a lot during the whole voyage.

  • Sonagirji
  • Kundalpur
  • Mukagirji
  • Bundelkhand
  • Sidhwarkut
  • Banediya
  • Kunthalgiri
  • Elora
  • Kachner
  • Pethan

& various beautiful Jain Temples!
