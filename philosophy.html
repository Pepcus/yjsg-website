Our Philosophies

Young Jain Study Group believes in the philosophies of Jainism.The philosophies of Jainism are:

  • Belief on independent existence of soul and matter.
  • Nobody is the creator, owner, preserver or destroyer of the universe. Every element is self-made.
  • Faith in the concept of Anekantwad & Syadwad
  • Potency of karma, eternal universe
  • The real happiness lies in liberation of soul.
  • Whatever we do result in Karmas as action and reaction: if we sow goodness, we will reap goodness & vice versa
