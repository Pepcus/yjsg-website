DIWALI AWARENESS CAMPAIGN

"There are better ways to spend money than burn it!"

YJSG Diwali Awareness Campaign aims to save the nature, money & health of the people. Hoardings & Banners are put on the road with a catchy message & picture. Students are told the various disadvantages so that they take a pledge of not cracking fire crackers.
